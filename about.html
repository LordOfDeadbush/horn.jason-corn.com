About the Instructor

I am currently studying at Santa Clara University with a double major in Music and Electrical Engineering. I also have a background in software engineering from studying at Foothill College.

I have been studying French Horn for 10 years, most of which I have played extensively with various orchestral, band and jazz groups. I also have a large amount of experience with solo repitoire for horn, including low horn and jazz horn.

I have been studying Trumpet for 5 years, with an emphasis in band repitoire. I also have experience with jazz trumpet.

As a high school graduate of the area, I have been very active in music performance in the area. Academically, I have been performing Horn with the SCU Wind Ensemble. I have also been a proud member of the SCU Bronco Pep Band, and am proud to be a member of the first band to perform live at a SF Giants game! This past spring, I also had the fantastic opportunity to perform with the Intercollegiate Band at the CBDNA West-Northwest conefrence.

Along with my work at school, I have also been a member of various community groups. Currently, I am working with Los Altos' Ye Olde Towne Band as one of their 2nd trumpet players. In the past I have also played with the De Anza performing arts department as a horn player.
